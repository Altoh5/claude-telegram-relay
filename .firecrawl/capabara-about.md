# About Capabara

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

![](<Base64-Image-Removed>)

Capabara is a revolutionary capability-as-a-service gen AI platform, designed to help SMEs instantly AI-enable their organisations.  We believe that AI should be accessible to every business, not just those with deep pockets, robust IT teams or massive infrastructure. That’s why we’ve built a powerful, no-code AI platform designed to help SMEs thrive in an AI-driven world.  Unlike other AI solutions that focus on a single industry, Capabara is flexible and customisable, adapting to your unique needs—whether you’re in HR, legal, education, or any other sector. Our platform brings AI’s transformative capabilities to your organisation without the need for expensive infrastructure or technical expertise.  With Capabara, you get a versatile AI partner that’s easy to integrate and ready to scale as your business grows. Capabara helps you streamline processes, make smarter decisions, and unlock new growth opportunities—all while keeping things simple, secure, and affordable.
