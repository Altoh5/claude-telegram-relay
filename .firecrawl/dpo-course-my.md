This comprehensive course is designed for business leaders and individuals who work in cross-functional teams and are responsible for enhancing their capabilities in the realm of processing personal information within an organisation. Whether you are a seasoned professional or new to data protection, this course equips you with the skills and knowledge required to excel in your role.

**LEARNING OUTCOMES**

- Guidance for DPOs to start on your organisation’s PDPA journey
- Learn the information lifecycle of personal data, and understand how personal data flows through your organisation’s business processes
- Manage compliance efforts using a structured Data Protection Management Programme
- Guidance & best practices from experienced data protection practitioners

* * *

**COURSE OVERVIEW**

**Module 1**

**Introduction**

- What is the Personal Data Protection Act
- Non-Application of the Act
- Data Protection Roles
- Definition of Personal Data
- Privacy vs Security

**Module 2**

**A Business Process and Information Life Cycle Perspective**

**Collection of Personal Data**

- General Principle – Consent
- Notice & Choice Principle

**Usage of Personal Data**

- General Principle - Lawful Processing / Non-Excessive
- Accuracy Principle

**Storage/Disposal of Personal Data**

- Security Principle
- Retention Principle

**Disclosure & Transfer of Personal Data**

- Disclosure Principle
- Access Principle

**Module 3**

**Governance of Personal Data**

- Rights of the individuals
- Penalties & Criminal Offences
- Offences by A Body Corporate
- Abetment & Attempt to Commit Offence

**Module 4**

**Operational Compliance Framework**

**Implementing the Data Protection Management Program**

- Appoint your Governance/Steering Committee
- Assess Risks Relating to Personal Data
- Protection against Risks with Policies, Processes & Actions
- Response Management
