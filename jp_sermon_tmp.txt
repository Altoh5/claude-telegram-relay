
## Overview: Sunday Sermon on Conquering Fear of Death Through the Blood of Jesus
- Pastor Joseph Prince delivers a full Sunday sermon (~86 min) at what appears to be New Creation Church (Singapore)
- Core thesis: The blood of Jesus permanently defeats the devil's power of death, frees believers from fear, and unlocks the \"Year of Much More\" (2026 theme)
- Key outcomes: Salvation prayer led, healing prayer for memory/brain conditions, \"sprinkling of the blood\" declaration over congregation worldwide
- Structured around Hebrews 2:14-16, Romans 3, Psalm 23, Psalm 66, and Philippians 1

- **Situation:** Prophetic vision given to Pastor Prince showed people in the *middle* of a storm on a vessel \u2014 not calm waters first

## Fear of Death: The Root of All Fears (Core Theological Argument)
- Hebrews 2:14-16 is the anchor text for the entire sermon
- **The mother of all fears:** Fear of flying, pandemic anxiety, disease phobia \u2014 all trace back to fear of death
  - \"It's not the fear of the aircraft, it's the fear of death\"
- **Jesus' solution:** Partook of flesh and blood \u2192 *through death*, destroyed the one who had power of death (the devil)
  - Used death as the weapon against death \u2014 like a judo throw
- **Result for believers:** Released from lifetime bondage to fear of death
- **Paul's example (Philippians 1):** \"To die is gain\" \u2014 Paul had such dominion over death he chose to *stay alive* for the church's sake
  - \"To depart and be with Christ is far better\" \u2014 but \"to abide in the flesh is more needful for you\"
  - Demonstrates: no fear of death = dominion over death

## The Devil's Original Function \u2014 Why He's the \"Agent of Death\"
- Lucifer was originally an archangel (Prince believes one of three: Gabriel, Michael, Lucifer)
- **Ezekiel 28:** God addresses the \"king of Tyrus\" \u2014 reveals Lucifer's original identity
  - \"You were in Eden... every precious stone your covering... the anointed cherub who covers\"
  - Lucifer = guardian/overseer of God's throne = guardian of God's righteousness and justice
- **Ark of the Covenant analogy:** Mercy seat with two cherubim = picture of God's throne in heaven
  - Cherubim = guardians of God's righteousness and justice
  - They execute judgment on any infringement of God's holiness
- **How he became agent of death:** Authority to execute God's decree \u2014 \"the soul that sins must die\" \u2014 was originally his function
  - He still operates with this \"old job mentality\" even after his fall
- **His limitation post-cross:** Cannot accuse believers before God anymore (Revelation: \"accuser of brethren is fallen\")
  - Jesus' blood on the mercy seat = devil's mouth sealed before God's throne
  - BUT: He can still accuse *you to yourself* \u2014 that's where the battle is now

## The Cross: Where Mercy and Justice Kissed
- The divine dilemma: God wants to show mercy, but cannot at the expense of His righteousness
  - Devil knows this \u2014 exploits it as accusation
- **Solution:** Jesus died as the innocent for the guilty
  - His infinite divine nature means one death covers all sin \u2014 past, present, future (to 2026 and beyond)
  - God's holiness AND mercy both fully expressed at the cross simultaneously
- **Romans 3:24-26 (key verse):** God set forth Jesus as a \"propitiation\" (mercy seat) \u2014 by His blood
  - \"That He might be *just* AND the *justifier*\" \u2014 not just merciful, but *righteous* in justifying you
  - God is not saying \"I pity you\" \u2014 He's saying \"I am *righteous* in making you righteous\"
  - Profound distinction: justification is an act of God's righteousness, not merely His mercy

## The Blood of Jesus \u2014 Continuous Cleansing (Practical Application)
- 1 John 1:7: \"The blood of Jesus cleanses us\" \u2014 Greek is **present continuous tense** = always cleansing, right now
  - Bad thought \u2192 immediate cleansing, not delayed
- **Hebrews 9 \u2192 Hebrews 10 logic:** If blood of bulls/goats purified the flesh, how much MORE does Christ's blood purge the *conscience*?
  - Once purged, conscience should have \"no more consciousness of sins\"
- **Old Testament pattern \u2014 blood then oil:** Priests anointed: blood on right ear, thumb, toe \u2192 *then* oil applied
  - Oil (Holy Spirit/anointing) always follows the blood
  - Practical implication: before praying for the sick, be blood-conscious \u2192 accuser silenced \u2192 anointing flows
- **Warning about new Bible versions:** Some remove \"blood\" references \u2014 Prince calls this enemy activity
- **Triune God in Hebrews 9:14:** \"Blood of Christ [Son], who through the eternal Spirit [Holy Spirit], offered himself to God [Father]\"

## Melchizedek: The Order of Blessing (Not Cursing)
- Levitical priesthood = blessing for obedience, cursing for disobedience
- **Melchizedek's order:** First words ever recorded = \"Blessed be Abraham\" \u2014 no cursing, only blessing
  - Jesus is \"priest forever after the order of Melchizedek\" (Psalms, quoted multiple times in Hebrews)
  - Therefore: first thing Jesus says of you = \"Blessed be [your name]\"
- **Melchizedek brought bread and wine to exhausted Abraham** = picture of Communion
  - Communion = Jesus ministering healing and refreshment to the tired/battle-worn
- **Abraham gave tithes (ma'aser)** \u2014 before the Law, untaught, Spirit-led
  - Hebrew root: *Asaf* = \"ten\" or \"rich\" \u2014 the rich is *in* the tithe
  - Abraham refused king of Sodom's offer: \"Lest you say you made me rich\" \u2014 chose God's way of wealth over devil's
- **Tithing stance:** \"Do I *have* to tithe? No. Do I *get* to tithe? Yes \u2014 it's a privilege\"
  - Taught at wrong level (without Melchizedek revelation) causes confusion in the body of Christ

## Discipleship vs. Sonship \u2014 Identity Clarification
- Triggered by interview question on grace vs. discipleship
- **Key point:** \"Disciple\" = Greek/Hebrew word for *student* (Talmudim) \u2014 every rabbi had students
  - Even Judas was a disciple \u2014 disciples were not yet sons before the cross
- **Pattern in Scripture:**
  - Gospels + Acts: \"disciples, disciples, disciples\"
  - All epistles (Paul, Peter, James, John, Jude): **zero** use of \"disciple\" \u2014 instead: \"beloved,\" \"sons,\" \"saints\"
- **Danger of over-emphasizing discipleship:** Makes Christians feel inferior, undermines identity as sons/beloved
  - \"When you truly know you are God's beloved, you are able to love more than anybody else\"
- **Correct identity:** Seed of Abraham \u2192 children of God \u2192 beloved \u2192 saints (sanctified ones)

## The Imagination/Inner Vision \u2014 How God Speaks
- Ephesians 1: \"Eyes of your understanding (dianoia) being enlightened (photizo)\"
  - *Dianoia* = imagination part of the mind; *photizo* = light shining on it
  - God writes His laws on our *dianoia* (Jeremiah's new covenant promise)
- **Jesus' modus operandi (John 5):** \"The Son can do nothing of Himself, but what He sees the Father do\"
  - Father showed Jesus He wanted to heal the impotent man at Bethesda \u2192 Jesus zeroed in
  - Sets the pattern: we can receive inner visions/impressions as God's guidance
- **Devil's counterfeit:** Corrupts the imagination with fear, pandemic anxiety, past trauma images
  - Advice: Know the headlines \u2014 don't binge on news that only discusses problems without solutions

## Memory/Brain Healing Prayer
- Testimony shared: woman had memory lapses, restored after prayer in service
- **Theological basis:** Adam named ALL animals (land, sea, sky) and remembered them all \u2014 pre-fall mind was extraordinary
  - Medical science: humans use only 1/10 of brain capacity
  - Prince's revelation: God gave the church exactly **9 gifts of the Spirit** to restore the other 9/10
- **Speaking in tongues research:** University of Pennsylvania study (non-Christian professor) found that when Christians pray in tongues, a different part of the brain activates \u2014 the part that **reduces stress**
  - Aligns with Isaiah: \"This is the rest and the refreshing\" (tongues)
- **Confession practice:** Prince taught his son Justin to confess \"I have the mind of Christ\" (1 Corinthians 2:16) from young
- **Prayer declared:** Resurrection of brain/memory, reversal of disease (Alzheimer's, dementia), renewal of youth for the brain

## Psalm 23 \u2014 Walking *Through*, Not Staying
- \"Yea, *though* I walk through\" \u2014 not \"when\" (definite) but \"though\" (conditional)
- Shadow of death = only a shadow; shadows cannot harm (shadow of a dog cannot bite)
- Jesus removed the sting of death (1 Corinthians 15) \u2014 \"who's afraid of a bee with no sting?\"
- Some believers will not die at all \u2014 caught up alive at Jesus' return (1 Thessalonians 4)
- **The cursed man (Jeremiah):** Like a heath in the desert \u2014 \"does not see when good comes\"
  - Too busy criticizing wife, children, circumstances to notice blessings
- **The blessed man:** \"Does not *see* when heat comes\" \u2014 too focused on good to experience the heat
  - Practical challenge: this week, look for the good in spouse, children, government, pastor

## Receiving from God \u2014 The Pharisee Problem
- Pool of Bethesda: many sick present, only ONE healed
- Capernaum: packed house, \"power of the Lord present to heal *them*\" (plural) \u2014 but only one healed
  - Pharisees were criticizing instead of receiving
- **Principle:** You cannot receive the benefit of anyone you are criticizing
- **Smoke analogy:** Some people who went through trials \u2014 \"you can smell the smoke before they even speak\"
  - Others: you can't tell they went through anything \u2014 they chose to glorify God
